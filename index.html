About Me


Hi, I'm Chris! I am currently a student at the Northwestern Coding Bootcamp. My first project was to make this website!

I am also a research technician, working in the stem cell core facility of Northwestern University. Using virus technology, reprogram blood cells from patients into stem cells so that researchers can study them. And using molecular biology technologies, I make mutant stem cells to study the role of genes in patient disease.

I received a Master in Business Administration from the National Louis University, a Master in Microbiology and Immunology from the Rosalind Franklin University of Medicine and Science, and a Bachelor of Science in Biology from the University of Missouri - Kansas City.
